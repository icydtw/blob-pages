Why Blob?

We believe that understanding your emotions is the first step to a healthier, more mindful life. Blob combines the simplicity of a journal with the power of AI to give you a deeper perspective on your emotional well-being.

Your Privacy Matters

Your entries are stored locally on your device. We don't sell your data, and we don't share it with third parties. Optional iCloud backup ensures your data is safe and accessible only to you.
